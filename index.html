Harness Nature's Healing Power

Founded in 2015, our journey began with a focus on sharing the benefits of aromatherapy and natural oils, which has evolved into a comprehensive approach to holistic wellness. At Aroma Base Limited, we are passionate about harnessing the power of nature to enhance the well-being and quality of life for individuals and families.

Our Offerings

Aromatherapy and natural products

We specialize in developing customized body care products that incorporate the therapeutic properties of essential oils. Our products are designed to promote relaxation, rejuvenation, and overall well-being. Through strategic partnerships with educational centres, we offer interest-oriented lessons and workshops, empowering enthusiasts with the knowledge to integrate aromatherapy into their daily lives. Our mission is to establish aromatherapy as a complementary therapy that enhances physical and mental health, fostering a more balanced lifestyle.

Thrive (algae oil) distribution

In addition to our aromatherapy offerings, Aroma Base Limited is a leading distributor of Thrive, high-quality algae oil in Macao. Rich in monounsaturated fatty acids, our algae oil is perfect for everything from dressings to baking, sautéing, and high-heat frying. Its light taste means it won’t overpower the other flavours of your food, providing a healthier alternative for culinary enthusiasts. This expansion into sustainable nutrition aligns with our commitment to promoting holistic well-being, ensuring that our customers can nourish their bodies with the same care and attention they devote to their mental and emotional health.
